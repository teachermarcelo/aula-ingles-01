📚 Grammar: Numbers in English

📖 Cardinal Numbers (1-100)

1-12 (Irregular)

one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, eleven, twelve

13-19 (-teen)

thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen

20-90 (-ty)

twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty, ninety

21-99 (Compound)

twenty-one, thirty-five, forty-two, ninety-nine

📖 Ordinal Numbers (1st, 2nd, 3rd...)

1st - first

2nd - second

3rd - third

4th - fourth

5th - fifth

...

100th - one hundredth

💡 Important Rules:

  • Hyphen: Use hyphen for 21-99 (twenty-one, not twenty one)
  • And: In British English: "one hundred and five"
  • No "and": In American English: "one hundred five"
  • Zero: "zero" or "oh" (for phone numbers)
