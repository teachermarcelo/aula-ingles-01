📚 Grammar: Numbers in English

📖 Cardinal Numbers (1-100)

1-12 (Irregular)

one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, eleven, twelve

13-19 (-teen)

thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen

20-90 (-ty)

twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty, ninety

21-99 (Compound)

twenty-one, thirty-five, forty-two, ninety-nine

📖 Ordinal Numbers

1st - first

2nd - second

3rd - third

4th - fourth

5th - fifth

10th - tenth

💡 Important Rules:

  • Hyphen: Use hyphen for 21-99 (twenty-one)
  • And: British: "one hundred and five" / American: "one hundred five"
